A Very Average Individual:
5 Minutes With Will Allen

Yesterday I had the pleasure to get an interview with the always intriguing and interesting William Allen. He took the time to talk to me after a hard faught Fort Knight game where he placed 5th.

What is your favorite food? Why?

“Pizza because it is very convenient and you can eat a lot of it at one time. Plus you can customize your favorite toppings on it. “

What is your favorite music? Why do you like it?

“Classic rock because my family got me into it at a young age.”

What is your favorite vacation place? Why do you have any specific memories that you can share?

“My favorite place to vacation is in Maine, some memories I have are water skiing when I was a kid.”

What is one thing in the world that you would like to accomplish (when you are older?)

“I want to have a family.”

What is one thing in the world that you would like to change?

“I would like to get challenge global warming by attacking C02 producing products and helping to make alternatives.”

What is one thing in the world that makes you most happy?

“My family.”
